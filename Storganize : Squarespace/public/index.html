Our boxes are standard, medium-sized cardboard moving boxes. The exact dimensions are 18' x 18' x 16' or 3 cubic ft.


We provide you with our boxes for free upon request, but if you prefer to use your own boxes or bins, were happy to accept them. All boxes are charged at the same rate as our boxes of $12/month (Study Abroad Only) per box regardless of size.

For how many months do you think you will store your stuff?

(most students store for 5 months)
Need Help?

Most students store for the Spring Semester, which is 5 months long from December - May.


Many students studying abroad tend to store for 9 months, from December - August.


In general, Storganize does have a 3 month minimum, but you can request your items back at any time. If you are storing for less than 3 months, you will be charged for the full 3 months for items you remove early.
